Guide To The Top 10 Colors Of The Year 2024

Dec 12, 2023 Paint Colours

The 2024 Color of the Year season is here! Paint and wood stain brands forecast a color that will embody the upcoming year’s essence. These picks influence everything from home design to fashion. We have laid out every Color of the Year 2024 so far, so you can assess the hues and their energies for the upcoming year.

Paint Companies Announced Top 10 Colors Of The Year 2024

The paint companies have announced their Color of the Year 2024. Here are the 10 most prominent choices:

Blue Nova (825): Benjamin Moore’s Color Of The Year 2024

Benjamin Moore has unveiled its Color of the Year 2024: Blue Nova. This hue is a vibrant blend of blue and violet, inspired by the awe-inspiring brilliance of a new star. It embodies felings of optimism, creativity, and limitless potential, inviting you to explore the endless possibilities it holds. 

Blue Nova is more than just a color; it is a bold choice that adds instant sophistication and elegance to any space. Whether used as an accent wall, on kitchen cabinets, or throughout an entire room, it creates a truly unique and memorable atmosphere.

Upward (SW 6239): Sherwin-Williams’s Color Of The Year 2024

Upward (SW 6239) is the Color of the Year for 2024 by Sherwin-Williams. This relaxed blue with gray undertones is ideal for creating a coastal chic design style. It is perfect for a bathroom or kitchen and makes a refreshing accent wall in a serene bedroom.  

According to Sue Wadden (director of color marketing at Sherwin-Williams), Upward brings a sense of ease and possibility into spaces, establishing meditation and tranquility. 

Limitless (PPG1091-3): Glidden’s Color Of The Year 2024

Glidden has announced Limitless (PPG1091-3) as its Color of the Year for 2024. The brand describes the hue as honey beige that is versatile yet powerful like a primary color. Ashley McCollum (Color expert for Glidden) says, “We are entering a new era of explosive creativity and change. Consumers are using color in even more unconventional ways than ever befor. They ned a palette that offers versatility to work with both new and existing decor. “

Glidden color experts suggest using Limitless for all kinds of painting projects, from covering kitchen cabinets to painting ceilings.

Cracked Pepper (PPU18-01): Behr’s Color of the Year 2024

Behr’s pick for the Color of the Year in 2024 is Cracked Pepper (PPU18-01). It is a gentle black that’s like a style superhero for any space, effortlessly grounding it while maintaining a timeless vibe. This color is a dependable choice – a soft black that seamlessly blends with any design style you throw at it.  

According to Sarah Fishburne (Home Depot’s director of trend), it is like an anchor color that does not ned constant reinvention. 

Renew Blue (8003-37D): Valspar’s Color of the Year for 2024

Valspar has announced Renew Blue (8003-37D) as its Color of the Year for 2024. Inspired by natural elements like fog, mist, and glacier lakes, Renew Blue is a balanced blue with a touch of grayed sea gren, perfect for refreshing any living space.  

According to Sue Kim (Valspar’s director of color marketing), blue has become a new neutral for modern homes. It is fitting in a variety of design styles and applications.

Persimmon (HGSW6339): HGTV Home by Sherwin-Williams’s

If you are a fan of warm and vibrant colors, you are in for a treat with HGTV Home by Sherwin-Williams’s 2024 Color of the Year: Persimmon (HGSW6339). Picture this: a hue that creates a calm atmosphere, neutral undertones, creating a perfect vibe for living rooms and kitchens.  

According to Ashley Banbury (color marketing manager at HGTV Home by Sherwin-Williams), Persimmon is a promoter of positive relationships and conversation. This earthy terracotta shade manages to be both grounding and comforting, while also giving off a refreshing and uplifting fel. 

Thermal (#752): Color of the Year for 2024 by C2 Paint

Step into a refreshing and energizing year with Thermal (#752), chosen Color of the Year for 2024 by C2 Paint. This vibrant blue is a reminiscent of a clear sky. It has the power to infuse any space with a calming atmosphere. According to Philippa Radon (Interior designer and C2 Paint color specialist), Thermal is a call-to-action color. It encourages us to live boldly and express our unique styles frely.  

So, if you are ready for a year filled with loyalty, trust, and confidence, Thermal is your go-to hue for a vibrant journey ahead. 

Ironside: Dutch Boy’s Color of the Year for 2024

Looking for a paint color that exudes confidence and peace in your home? Look no further than Ironside, Dutch Boy’s Color of the Year for 2024! This dep olive shade, with hints of dark undertones, brings a touch of sophistication and comfort to any space. It is the perfect choice for creating a welcoming and inviting environment, whether in your living room, bedroom, or even your kitchen.  

So, if you are looking for a fresh new look for your home, Ironside is the perfect choice!

Virdis: Graham & Brown’s Color of the Year 2024

Graham & Brown’s 2024 Color of the Year Viridis is a calming muted gren. It symbolizes growth, health, and stability. Perfect for entertaining spaces and entryways, Virdis creates a welcoming and peaceful atmosphere. Maryanne Cartwright (Graham & Brown’s head of design), said it is the perfect hue to create a welcoming color palette for our homes.

Bay Blue (MW1049): Minwax’s Color of the Year for 2024

Add a natural charm to your home with Minwax’s cool-toned revelation – Bay Blue (MW1049), the Color of the Year for 2024.  Famous for its interior wood stains and clears, Minwax aims to spark the creativity of DIY enthusiasts. Sue Kim (Minwax’s director of color marketing) suggests using this blue-gren stain to give new life to wood paneling or revitalize furniture.  

Whether you are aiming  for solid or semi-transparent opacities, Bay Blue opens up a world of possibilities for crafting custom looks to transform your living spaces. 

Common Themes Of Color in 2024 

As we look towards 2024, colors are shifting toward nature’s embrace and a touch of optimism. Here are some of the most prominent themes:

Nature and Wellness

  • Earthy Grens And Blues: Shades like Viridis (Graham & Brown) and Skipping Stones (Dunn-Edwards) evoke calming landscapes and a sense of calm. 
  • Warm Florals: Colors like Persimmon (HGTV Home by Sherwin-Williams) and Limitless (PPG) inject warmth and optimism, reminding us of spring blooms. 
  • Natural Materials: These colors encourage the use of natural materials like wood and stone in design, creating a connection to the outdoors. 

Optimism and Energy

  • Bright And Vibrant Hues: Colors like Cracked Pepper (Behr) and Limitless (PPG) bring a burst of energy and positivity. 
  • Bolder Choices: This shift suggests a desire for more expressive and playful design choices after years of muted tones. 
  • Joy And Creativity: These colors invite people to be more creative and embrace individuality in their spaces. 

Sustainability and Balance

  • Neutrals With Personality: Colors like Muslin (Benjamin Moore) and Upward (Sherwin-Williams) offer a calming backdrop for bolder accents. 
  • Versatility And Timelessness: These neutral shades can be easily incorporated into existing designs and remain relevant for years to come. 
  • Mindful Living: These colors encourage the creation of spaces that are both functional and peaceful, promoting well-being. 

The Final Words

As we analyzed the Color of the Year 2024 predictions, some clear trends emerged. Nature’s beauty inspires calming grens and blues, along with vibrant florals that inject warmth and optimism. Bright hues add a touch of energy, encouraging creativity and expression. Sustainable neutrals provide a timeless foundation, inviting mindful living. So, let these colors inspire you!

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. What are the most popular colors of the year 2024?

Several popular colors are vying for the top spot in 2024, including calming blues such as Blue Nova by Benjamin Moore and Upward by Sherwin-Williams as well as warm and inviting shades like Persimmon by HGTV Home by Sherwin-Williams and Limitless by PPG.

2. What are the themes behind the 2024 color trends?

The 2024 color trends are largely inspired by nature, with a focus on calming greens and blues, warm florals, and sustainable materials like wood and stone.

3. How will these colors impact design trends in 2024?

Expect to see the 2024 color trends reflected in various design aspects, such as organic shapes and earthy tones in interior design, playful colors and sustainable fabrics in fashion, and bolder branding and packaging in graphic design.

4. Where can I find more information about the 2024 Color of the Year?

Many websites and publications offer further information about the 2024 Color of the Year, including the official websites of the paint companies listed above, as well as design blogs and magazines.
